About Us

Since 1999, The Garrison's Café has been a cherished part of Birmingham's community. Known for our warm atmosphere and quality food, we offer a diverse menu featuring locally sourced ingredients. From hearty breakfasts to delicious lunches and decadent desserts, each dish is prepared with care by our passionate team. Whether you're catching up with friends, hosting a meeting, or enjoying a quiet moment, The Garrison's is the perfect spot to relax and indulge. Join us and experience why we've been a favorite for over two decades.

We take pride in sourcing fresh ingredients and providing exceptional service.
